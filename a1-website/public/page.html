Get to know us deeply

Hi! I'm Yasine Grace and Jolly, two passionate advocate for diversity in technology. From a young age, we were fascinated by the power of technology to transform lives. Our journey in the tech world started with a simple curiosity and has grown into a mission to empower women in the ever-evolving field of technology.

We believe that everyone, regardless of gender, should have equal opportunities to thrive in the tech industry. Through Women in Tech, we aim to break barriers and shape a future where diversity is not just celebrated but embraced as a driving force for innovation.

Join us on this exciting journey as we explore the vast possibilities that technology offers and work towards creating a more inclusive and diverse tech community.

Yasine Grace
Senior Technology Writer
Yasine Grace is a Tech enthusiast with a passion to empower women to join technlogy. she has a background in Software Technology.
